Exercise that could be chosen based on personnel preference.

Simple Exercise

Exercise that could be done easily, indoor and multiple times

1 minutes of plank will burn about 3 to 4 calories

20 minutes session of pilates will burn 83 to 150 calories

10 push up will burn on average 3.2 calories

20 squats will burn on average 6.4 calories

Every hour of biking will usually burn around 300 to 600 calories

Depends on the person a general outdoor circuit will burn around 240 to 360 calories
